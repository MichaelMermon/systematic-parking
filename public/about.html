About Us

Welcome to the Car Parking System. Our platform is designed to provide an efficient and convenient parking management solution, ensuring a seamless experience for users.

Our mission is to simplify parking reservations and monitoring through user-friendly technology. We aim to reduce the hassle of finding parking slots by offering real-time availability updates and easy-to-use reservation options.

Feel free to explore and enjoy a more organized parking experience with us. Your satisfaction is our priority.

Reservation Receipt Functionality

After successfully reserving a parking slot, users will receive a downloadable receipt in PDF format. This receipt includes the following details:

  • Your Name and Contact Information
  • Assigned Parking Slot ID
  • Reservation Start and End Time (formatted for readability)

Please ensure to download and save the receipt. Present it at the parking lot entrance for verification. The receipt also includes instructions for parking procedures and serves as proof of your reservation.
